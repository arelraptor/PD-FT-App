Registration and Access

To use the tool, you must first register an account. Once registered, you can log in to the system using either your username or your email address. Both methods are equally valid for authentication to ensure you can always access your videos and results easily.

How to upload a video

Go to the "Upload" section, select your video file, and provide a title. While MP4 is the preferred format, the system also accepts other common extensions (such as MOV, AVI, or MKV). If your video is not an MP4, it will be automatically converted during the upload process to ensure full compatibility. Once uploaded, the system will process the video to detect movement patterns.

Understanding Status Icons
  • Processing: The AI is currently analyzing your video.
  • Success: Analysis complete. You can now see the prediction.
  • Error: Something went wrong (e.g., hand not detected).
Prediction Scores (0-3+)

The prediction indicates the severity of the movement disorder based on clinical scales:

  • 0: Normal: Fast, large movements with consistent rhythm.
  • 1: light: Mild slowing or reduction in amplitude.
  • 2: Mild: Moderate slowing/reduction; early, temporary fatiguing.
  • 3+: Moderate: Marked slowing/reduction; consistent fatigue
